## Question 1

The input is a word and the output is the word printed in the X cross format or the text "Invalid word input".
### For example:
| **Input**     | **Result** |
|:--------------|:-----------|
|hello|h&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;o<br>&nbsp;e&nbsp;l&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;l&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;e&nbsp;l&nbsp;<br>h&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;o|
|love|Invalid word input|
