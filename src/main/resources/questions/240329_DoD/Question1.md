## Question 1
Write a program to output the century from the given year.

The input is the year, and the output is century with its suffix.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|1500|15th|
|1535|16th|
|2021|21st|
|2245|23rd|
|2200|22nd|
|1400|14th|
|1985|20th|
|3021|31st|
|3215|33rd|
|2105|22nd|
|1201|13th|
