## Question 5
Write a program to find the quotient and remainder of two integers.

The input is two integers: Dividend and Divisor. The output is the quotient and the remainder.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|10 3|3 1|
|20 5|4 0|
|5 2|2 1|
|100 10|10 0|
