## Question 12
Write a program that prints the first 10 multiples of a number.

The input is a single integer N. The output is the first 10 multiples of N, separated by spaces.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|3|3 6 9 12 15 18 21 24 27 30|
|5|5 10 15 20 25 30 35 40 45 50|
