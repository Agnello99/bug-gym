## Question 4
Calculate the power of a number (Base^Exponent) using a loop.

The input consists of two integers: Base and Exponent (exponent >= 0). The output is the result of Base raised to the power of Exponent.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|2 3|8|
|5 2|25|
|3 0|1|
|10 2|100|
|2 10|1024|
