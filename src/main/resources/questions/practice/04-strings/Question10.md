## Question 10
Toggle the case of every character in a string.
Convert uppercase to lowercase and lowercase to uppercase. Numbers/symbols remain unchanged.

The input is a string. The output is the toggled string.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|Hello|hELLO|
|Java|jAVA|
|123|123|
|A|a|
