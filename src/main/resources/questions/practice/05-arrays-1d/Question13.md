## Question 13
Count the number of Even and Odd integers in an array.
Input starts with N (size), followed by N integers. Output the count of Even numbers, followed by the count of Odd numbers.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|5 <br> 1 2 3 4 5|2 3|
|4 <br> 2 4 6 8|4 0|
|4 <br> 1 3 5 7|0 4|
