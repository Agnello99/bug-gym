## Question 3
Find the index of a specific number in an array (Linear Search).

The input starts with an integer N (size). Then N integers follow. Finally, a Target integer is provided.
The output is the index (0-based) of the first occurrence of the Target. If the target is not found, output -1.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|5 <br> 10 20 30 40 50 <br> 30|2|
|3 <br> 1 2 3 <br> 5|-1|
|5 <br> 5 5 5 5 5 <br> 5|0|
|4 <br> 10 20 30 40 <br> 40|3|
