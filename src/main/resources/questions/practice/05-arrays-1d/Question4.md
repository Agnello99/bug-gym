## Question 4
Count how many times a specific number appears in an array.

The input starts with an integer N (size). Then N integers follow. Finally, a Target integer is provided.
The output is the number of times Target appears in the array.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|6 <br> 1 2 3 2 4 2 <br> 2|3|
|5 <br> 1 1 1 1 1 <br> 1|5|
|3 <br> 10 20 30 <br> 50|0|
|4 <br> 5 10 5 10 <br> 10|2|
