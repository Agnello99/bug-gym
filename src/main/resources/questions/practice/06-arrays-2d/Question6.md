## Question 6
Count number of occurrences of a specific element in a 2D matrix.

The input starts with R (Rows) and C (Columns), followed by R*C matrix elements, and finally a Target integer.
The output is the count of how many times the Target appears.

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|2 3 <br> 1 2 1 <br> 3 1 4 <br> 1|3|
|2 2 <br> 5 5 <br> 5 5 <br> 5|4|
|3 1 <br> 10 <br> 20 <br> 30 <br> 99|0|
