## Question 7
Print the Transpose of a matrix.
(Rows become columns, and columns become rows).

The input starts with R (Rows) and C (Columns), followed by matrix elements.
The output should be the elements of the transposed matrix (C x R).

### For example:
| **Input** | **Result** |
|:--------- |:-----------|
|2 3 <br> 1 2 3 <br> 4 5 6|1 4 2 5 3 6|
|2 2 <br> 1 2 <br> 3 4|1 3 2 4|
|1 3 <br> 10 20 30|10 20 30|
